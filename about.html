About Me

Hello there, my name is Terrence Coy. I have a degree in Computer Science from UNC Greensboro and I enjoy making 3D prototypes and web applications.

During my free time I like to come up with ideas for new projects or help others with their own. I enjoy collaborating with others and bouncing ideas around to make something new. When I am away from the computer I like to fabricate and fix things, go backpacking, hunt for fossils and do research for my zine.

Currently I am looking for full-time work as a software developer but I would be open to collaborating on a personal project as well. Whether it be asset creation or programming feel free to reach out.
